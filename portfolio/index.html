The Game

The Game was my favourite project. It challenged us creatively in many different areas, game design, UI design, illustration, coding and sound production. The assignement was to make a point and click type of game where the player would avoid bad objects and click on the good ones. Since I am experienced in coding I made it more complex than requried to challenge myself in this area. I learned a lot about sound design and the basics of Adobe Illustrator along with illustration itself where I am not very comfortable.

Photography tutorial

The focus of this project was to create a design, UX and a responsive website with the topic of Photography tutorial and given content. Before designing, each of us recieved a specific design style according to which we would have to model the design and everything around it. My style was deconstructivism which I grew to like in the proccess of learning about it and designing the website.

Design sprint

The design sprint was our first group project, our assignement was to create a concept of a native app that would improve the life of KEA students. My role was to design the whole app along with Alexandra. We did all of the work in Adobe XD and learned how to use it properly and how to make a working prototype. Our app idea was to make a platform where students would have an overview of all of the KEA workshops and easily register for them.

A story without words

In this project we had to create a short 1 minute video that would tell a story without using any words. The additional constraint to this was that it had to be filmed on a phone camera. The most challenging part for me was to come up with the story. After a lot of thinking I made a video where the main character appears in a forest and starts running when he sees a monster, the video ends with an unexpected turn. I learned a lot about filming, camera angles, editing in Adobe Premiere and I also dipped my toes in After Effects.
